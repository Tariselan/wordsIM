8.

I adore the rain.
J'adore the sounds it makes against my window,
the way is ceases all sense of disaster.
J'adore the way the light softens on the word.
What I like most about it now? Is the way it reminds me of them.
The way they both seem to make the world better,
more peaceful,
calmer,
happier.
But one thing sets them apart; she makes the world brighter.
Whereas the clouds darken the sky,
her voice & presence only brighten the room.
Where the cold pouring rain leaves eventually from my head,
He never leaves from mine hart and their warm glow seems
to keep me like a wee lad near a fire.
I love her so, no matter how words may wander,
how geese may gander, nor Danes be invaded.
Forever shall I love & yearn for a way to tell you,
truly just how much I love you.

12. - 3rd of June 2022

A light flickers in the shimmering sky,
the wind and sea speak close to my window.
I wish to care for thee, look after thee,
to make sure all for thee is perfect.
Alas, distance 'tyet time keeps me away.
The spots of dead, the rides of long,
the passage of time,
I would go through them infinitely many times,
if it only meant to see thee but one more time.
Pour moi? C'est pas la mer, c'est pas le mort.
Ce n'est pas rien that stop me seeing you
ut I could make you,
just a little bit happier.
