3.

Perfection is in the eye of the beholder.
But true perfection stays all but wothout character
My love does not fall for what myne eyes may see

5.

un petit peu de joyeux
every day, thunder or shine, light falls upon my face.

6.

I dreamt once, now I also dream.
I used to think I saw things a near,
but now I hear the colours with my eyes
Such a life I lived defies so much to the life I must live
A match stricken, set aflame, is not put in a box
why shouldst I?
Thou wilt say unto me, 'but why, why dost thou say as such?'
and I will give thee an answer,
'out of love, my dear, out of love.'
